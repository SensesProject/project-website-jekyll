The Climate Change Scenario Toolkit

The Toolkit will be tailor-made and user determined connecting the new generation of climate community scenarios to decisive stakeholder groups. Complex scenario information is translated into relevant knowledge for these user groups to gain insights into

  • adaptation to climate change
  • mitigation of climate change and
  • residual climate impacts

The Toolkit will include

  • a unique set of user-centered scenario visualization tools, and
  • practical guidelines and manuals on co-production techniques of relevant climate change scenario knowledge.
